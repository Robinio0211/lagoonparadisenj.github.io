About this Rental

From the sunrise on the lagoon to the sunset on the bay, you will enjoy an exceptional experience , from swimming in the outdoor private pool or re-energizing in the outdoor hot tub to relaxing on the beach. Located in a quiet neighborhood, this spacious 4/5-bedroom house gives a direct access to the lagoon and complementary access to the beach. The house also offers a large office and free WiFi. Complementary access to Ocean Gate beach. Options to rent 4 adult bikes (with helmets) and 4 one-person kayaks (with paddles and life jackets). The pool is open from April to November. The hot tub can be used all year long.
